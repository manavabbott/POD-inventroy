Generate Unique Piece Barcodes
Bas barcodes banenge — stock abhi add nahi hoga. Jab scan ADD mode mein karoge, tabhi piece stock mein aayega. Har piece ek baar hi scan ho sakta hai.
Sample barcode format
HD-M-000001-A7K9
Har piece ka unique barcode, sequential + random combo
How it works (Full Flow)
Step 1: Generate karo (e.g. 50 Hoodie M barcodes) — stock abhi +0 hi rahega
Step 2: "Print Labels" → stickers nikalo aur pieces pe chipka do
Step 3: Jab pieces ready ho jaayen (stock mein aayen), Scanner → ADD mode → har piece scan → stock +1 per scan
Step 4: Jab print/dispatch ho, Scanner → MINUS mode → scan → stock -1 per scan
Benefit: Har piece ek baar ADD hota hai, ek baar MINUS. Dubara scan = "Already scanned" error. 100% cheating-proof.
